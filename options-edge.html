📖 EARNINGS VOLATILITY PLAYBOOK
Before earnings, implied volatility rises consistently. After earnings, IV collapses instantly (IV crush). The speculative call strategy exploits pre-earnings IV expansion — buy low IV, ride it higher into the event, exit before the number drops.

01
IV Percentile — Buy below 40% of 52-week range. Above 70% = avoid.
02
Bid/Ask Spread — Below 5% of mark price. Above 15% = red flag.
03
DTE vs Earnings — Expire 7–14 days after earnings. Expiring before = miss the catalyst.
04
Vega/Theta Ratio — Above 1.0. Below 1.0 = theta winning.
05
Liquidity — OI >500, Volume >100 (soft mins).
06
Strike Reachability — Within historical 1-SD earnings move.
07
Exit Discipline — SELL BEFORE EARNINGS PRINT. Target 50–100% gain or day before earnings, whichever first.
